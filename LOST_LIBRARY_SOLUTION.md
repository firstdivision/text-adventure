# The Lost Library - Solution Guide

## How to Complete "The Lost Library" Adventure

### Part 1: Exploration & Key Discovery

**Objectives:**
- Explore the entrance
- Collect initial items
- Gather clues

**Actions:**
1. `look` - Examine the current room
2. `examine desk` - See what's there
3. `take brass key` - Pick up the brass key
4. `take ledger` - Pick up the ledger
5. `read ledger` - Read the entry mentioning "the looking glass"

**Key Clue:** *"Section 7 contains restricted materials. The way forward is through the looking glass."*

### Part 2: Upper Gallery Discovery

**Objectives:**
- Navigate to the Upper Gallery
- Find the magical mirror
- Reveal the secret exit

**Actions:**
1. `go north` - Go to Main Stacks
2. `go up` - Climb to Upper Gallery
3. `look` - View the room
4. `examine mirror` - Examine the ornate mirror
5. **The mirror reveals the hidden exit to the west!**

**What Happens:** After examining the mirror, you'll see a system message revealing a doorway to the west that was previously hidden.

### Part 3: Additional Exploration

**Objectives:**
- Collect more clues and items
- Prepare for the vault

**Things to Try:**
- `go south` (from entrance) - Basement is locked (need rusty key)
- `go east` (from entrance) - Reading room with newspaper
- `examine newspaper` - Learn about "Mirror of Shadows" legend
- `go south` (from reading room) - Vault is locked (need brass key)

### Part 4: The Vault

**Objectives:**
- Unlock and enter the vault
- Discover the ancient scroll
- Uncover the library's secrets

**Actions:**
1. From entrance: `go east` â†’ Reading room
2. `go south` - Vault (requires brass key)
3. `look` - Examine the vault
4. `examine case` - Try to examine the glass case
5. `take scroll` - Take the ancient scroll
6. `read scroll` - Read the scroll's message

**What You Learn:** The library was built as a repository of lost knowledge. A final chamber holds a gift for the worthy.

### Part 5: The Hidden Chamber (Basement)

**Objectives:**
- Access the basement
- Find the rusty key
- Reach the hidden chamber

**Actions:**
1. From entrance: `go south` - Basement (requires rusty key)
2. To get rusty key: Go to basement archives first
   - (Note: Currently basement and hidden chamber aren't complete)
3. Once you have rusty key, go south from basement
4. `look` - Examine the chamber
5. `take crystal` - Take the glowing crystal
6. `examine crystal` - Complete your journey

### Puzzle Solutions

#### Puzzle 1: Access the Vault
- **Problem:** Vault door requires brass key
- **Solution:** Take brass key from entrance desk

#### Puzzle 2: Find the Hidden Exit
- **Problem:** "The way forward is through the looking glass"
- **Solution:** Go to Upper Gallery and examine the mirror

#### Puzzle 3: Access the Basement
- **Problem:** Basement door is locked
- **Solution:** Find rusty key in basement archives (or look for it elsewhere)

#### Puzzle 4: Understand the Story
- **Problem:** What is this place?
- **Solution:** Read the ledger, newspaper, and ancient scroll

### Easter Eggs & Hidden Details

- The mirror's reflection shows things that aren't there initially
- The newspaper mentions the "Mirror of Shadows" legend
- The library has ancient symbols and mysteries
- Multiple interactive objects tell the story

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Forgetting to examine things** - Many clues are in examination text
2. **Not reading items** - The ledger contains the key hint
3. **Not looking in rooms** - Features and exits are described in `look` output
4. **Trying locked doors without items** - Get the brass key and rusty key first
